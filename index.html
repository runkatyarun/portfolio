My Story

While testing functionality of different applications, I was always curious as to whom this functionality was targeted. So, I started reading about User Experience 5 years ago.

To get a taste of it, I took a few classes in UX which ignited a passion to change the direction of my career.

As of today, I’ve run dozens of user test sessions, presented the results to different teams and iterated multiple times in prototypes design.

To further my abilities in this field, I attended a User-Centered Design Certificate Course from the University of Washington.

How my background helps me in UX?

I have had experience with several different platforms such as mobile, desktop, web apps, and server-side. I know these platform’s abilities, limitations and main patterns.

As a good functional tester, I’m proficient in grooming each feature from general business requirements to the smallest detail. I pride myself on the ability to constantly think out of box.

Such skills have helped me in designing UX test plans.
