Prototype rules:

 

Please consider that this is a prototype and some elements may not be "clickable". The prototype is detailed enough to understand the interaction, main functional areas and elements placements.

 

If you see the cursor is changing on an element hover, it means you can click it.

 

I have also included some error scenarios, so there are some forms that will need to be submitted with caution.

 

 

Some details on the workflow:

 

CrowdExchange Project Manager (PM) may have one or more projects that he owns. He can create activities under these projects (tasks) that he may hold in a draft mode for a while or publish to the community.

 

Once an activity is published, PM will search and invite new developers to it. Developers on their side can register for the activities too.

Later, PM will decide who will work on his activity and assign one developer to it.

 

From time to time PM will explore if there are any activities that may need his reaction (e.g. not delivered in time) or just check status of the activities under development.

He can discuss all the issues in the activity comments thread or via the private message and make decisions based on it.
